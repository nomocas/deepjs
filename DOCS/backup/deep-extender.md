deep-extender
==========================
 
Deep Extender : recursivly analyse an object and seek after any "backgrounds" or "$ref" properties : dereference them and merge them with current extended or dereferenced property (the property where the backgrounds or $ref where found. - could be root).

It generalise the backgrounds/reference mecanism of the json-schema.
